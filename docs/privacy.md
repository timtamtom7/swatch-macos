# Swatch Privacy Policy

**Last updated: 2026-03-25**

## Overview

Swatch is a color picker and palette management tool for macOS. Your privacy is protected.

## Data Collection

Swatch does not collect any personal data.

**What we don't collect:**
- No analytics or tracking
- No user behavior data
- No device information
- No usage statistics

## Local Data Storage

All your data stays on your Mac:
- **Color History**: Stored locally in `~/Library/Application Support/Swatch/`
- **Palettes**: Saved locally with optional iCloud sync
- **Settings**: Stored in UserDefaults

## iCloud Sync

If iCloud sync is enabled:
- Only palette data syncs via your Apple ID
- Apple does not have access to your palette content
- You can disable iCloud sync anytime in Settings

## Widgets

The Swatch widget extension:
- Reads only from the shared app group container
- Does not transmit any data
- Shows your Color of the Day and saved palettes

## Shortcuts Integration

When using Swatch with macOS Shortcuts:
- Only reads/writes colors you explicitly select
- No background data collection

## Third Parties

Swatch does not use:
- Analytics SDKs
- Ad networks
- Data brokers
- Any third-party services

## Children

Swatch is not directed at users under 13.
